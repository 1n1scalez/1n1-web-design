# Most Outbound Sales Strategies are Broken

Let me guess your current outbound process:
1. Manually search for prospects on LinkedIn
2. Send a few connection requests
3. Copy-paste a generic message
4. Hope they respond

**This doesn't scale.**

You need a system that generates pipeline *while you sleep*.

## The Anatomy of an Automated Outbound Engine

### Layer 1: Multi-Channel Prospecting

You can't rely on just one channel. Your system should hit prospects across:
- **Cold Email** (1,000+ personalized emails per day)
- **LinkedIn Automation** (50-100 connections + follow-ups daily)
- **Retargeting Ads** (staying top-of-mind for engaged prospects)

Each channel feeds into the same CRM, creating a unified view of every touchpoint.

### Layer 2: The Qualification Filter

Here's the truth: **most people who respond aren't a fit.**

Your system needs to filter leads *before* they waste your time:

1. Prospect responds → Auto-reply with a qualification form
2. Form completed → Lead score calculated automatically
3. High score → Calendar booking link sent
4. Low score → Nurture sequence activated

**Result?** You only talk to people who can actually buy.

### Layer 3: Follow-Up on Steroids

The fortune is in the follow-up. Most deals don't close on the first touch—they close on touch #7.

Your automation should:
- Send follow-up emails on a schedule (Day 3, Day 7, Day 14)
- Track engagement (opens, clicks, replies)
- Escalate hot leads to your sales team instantly
